About Me

Hi! I'm Alyson, and I'm an Information Technology student at the University of Central Florida. I'm also an aspiring web developer and a plant enthusiast. I took an internet applications class in the Fall of 2022 and have since been honing my web development skills. Interestingly, the first two websites I developed were about plants. I took a break from web development soon after to focus more on school and my houseplant hobby, but now I'm back on the grind. I'm building this portfolio website to help me improve my understanding of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Sass.

Over the Summer of 2023, I began to build a blog website that I would use to document my journey as a plant enthusiast and showcase some of my favorite plants. While it is still in development as I improve my skills, I hope to publish the first iteration of my blog, Franco's Flora™, by the end of 2023.
